About Us

Just like any business venture. It all started with an idea. CYROK E-Market was created to provide a ‘one-stop-grocery-shop’ to consumers, however, I’m sure you have heard that statement before, so what makes us different? CYROK's goal is to provide customers with a convenient online shopping experience from anywhere in Jamaica. Covid-19 pandemic has changed the world in more ways than one. Consumers are relying on the digital world now more than ever and businesses are forced to adapt their strategies and shift toward digital transformation. CYROK E-Market facilitates multiple supermarkets and grocery retailers, providing customers with a convenient means of shopping from the comfort of their homes at any supermarket of their choice

Vision

To provide our customers the ability to shop online from the comfort of their homes and have access to multiple supermarkets or retail stores in their area at their fingertips.

Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with top-notch convenient services. We aim to eliminate the need for long lines in the supermarkets,promote social distancing, and have customers do all their shopping from the comfort of their homes at any supermarket of their choice.
